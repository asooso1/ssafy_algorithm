## 5-2번

![수와표현_5](2_수와 표현.assets/수와표현_5.PNG)

  

$$
y = 3log(x+3)+1              >>>
y-1 = 3log(x+3)              >>>
\frac{y-1}{3} =log(x+3)      >>>
10^{\frac{y-1}{3}} = x+3     >>>
10^{\frac{y-1}{3}}-3 = x     >>>
f^{-1}(x) = 10^{\frac{x-1}{3}}-3
$$

$$
f^{-1}(x) = 10^{\frac{x-1}{3}}-3
$$
